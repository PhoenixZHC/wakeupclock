Privacy Policy

Wakeup Clock - Smart Alarm App
Last Updated: January 2026

üîí Privacy Commitment

We take your privacy very seriously. Wakeup Clock does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to external servers. All data is stored locally on your device only.

1. Data Collection

This app only stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Alarm Settings: Alarm times, repeat modes, labels, and other configuration
  • Wake-up Records: Your daily wake-up times and streak statistics
  • App Settings: Theme mode, language preferences, anti-snooze settings

2. Permission Usage

This app requires the following system permissions:

  • Alarm Permission (AlarmKit): Used to create and manage system-level alarms
  • Background Audio: Used to play alarm sounds when triggered

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not:

  • Upload your data to any server
  • Share your data with any third parties
  • Use your data for advertising or analytics

4. Data Security

Your data security is protected by iOS's sandbox mechanism. Each app's data is isolated.

5. Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

  • In-app: Go to Settings ‚Üí Reset Data to clear all records
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the app will delete all locally stored data

6. Children's Privacy

This app does not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13. Content Rating: 4+.

7. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
