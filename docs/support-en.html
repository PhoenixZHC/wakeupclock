FAQ

Q: What if the alarm doesn't ring?

Please ensure alarm permission is granted. Go to Settings ‚Üí Wakeup Clock ‚Üí Alarms.

Q: How do I turn off the alarm?

Tap "Unlock Alarm" button, then complete a random mission (like math problems) to stop it.

Q: What is Anti-Snooze Mode?

After completing tasks, the system sends periodic reminders to confirm you're awake.

Q: Will the alarm override Silent mode?

Yes! Using AlarmKit, alarms automatically override Silent, DND, and Focus modes.

Q: How do I skip holidays?

Enable "Skip Public Holidays" option when adding an alarm.

Tips

  • Charge overnight: Low battery may cause automatic shutdown
  • Choose the right mission: Math missions are best for waking up
  • Use Anti-Snooze Mode: Recommended for users who tend to fall back asleep
